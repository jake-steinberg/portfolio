My name is Jake and I tell visual stories with maps, text, graphics, illustration, and code. My interests include data journalism, visual storytelling and all manner of mapmaking. I will graduate with an M.S. in Cartography and GIS from the University of Wisconsin-Madison in May. I will join the Wall Street Journal as a graphics intern in summer 2023.

I muse about maps @thesilentcarto and can be easily reached via email. You can also fork my code on Github and maybe even connect with me on LinkedIn. When I'm not working on a project, you can usually find me on my bike.
