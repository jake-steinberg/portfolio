I'm a Minneapolis-based cartographer and journalist. I work on the graphics team at the Minnesota Star Tribune, where I tell stories with maps and do GIS wizardry. I'm an editor for the NACIS Atlas of Design. I also do freelance map design. Interested? You can reach me at jakesteinberg96 at gmail.com.

When not mapping you might find me biking, rock climbing, fly fishing, or otherwise outside with Juneau, the American dingo.
