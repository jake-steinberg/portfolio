My name is Jake and I tell visual stories with maps, text, graphics, illustration, and code. I have a master's degree in cartography and GIS from the University of Wisconsin-Madison and spent a summer on the Wall Street Journal graphics team. I currently make graphics for the Minneapolis Star Tribune.

I muse about maps @thesilentcarto and can be reached at jakesteinberg96 at gmail.com. You can fork my code on Github and maybe even connect with me on LinkedIn. When I'm not working on a project, you can usually find me on my bike.
