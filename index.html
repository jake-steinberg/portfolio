My name is Jake and I tell visual stories with maps, graphics and code. I have a master's degree in cartography and GIS from the University of Wisconsin-Madison. My work has appeared in the Wall Street Journal and the NACIS Atlas of Design. I currently work on the graphics team at the Minneapolis Star Tribune. You can reach me at jakesteinberg96 at gmail.com.
