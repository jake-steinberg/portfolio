Toolkit

  • I'm proficient with QGIS and ArcGIS Pro to analyze and process data.

  • I design static maps with the Adobe suite, primarily Illustrator and Photoshop. I use ai2html to make my maps look good online.

  • I use Blender to generate realistic relief shading and pursue other 3D exploits.

  • JavaScript is my bread and butter for front-end web development. I build most of my apps with Node.js and Svelte.

  • I'm fluent in common mapping libraries, such as Leaflet, Mapbox GL JS and ArcGIS Maps SDK for JavaScript. I design custom basemaps with Mapbox Studio.

  • D3 and Turf.js are my go-tos for web data visualization and processing.
